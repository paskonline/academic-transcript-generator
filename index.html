System Guidelines & Formulas

1. Grade: The specific letter grade earned for each module.

2. Credit: Verify module credits via your degree handbook or department.

3. Grade PTS: Represents Quality Points (Grade Value × Credits).

4. Grading System: Customise the GPA values via the navbar menu.

• Total Credit Earned = Σ(Credits)
• Total Quality Points = Σ(Grade Points × Credits)
• Cumulative GPA = Total Quality Points / Total Credit Earned

Note: Data is processed locally. We do not store your information.

Import Instructions

To bulk upload your results, ensure your Excel or CSV file contains a Header Row with the following names:

  • Module Name (Compulsory)
  • Result (Compulsory - e.g., A, B+, C)
  • Year (Optional)
  • Semester (Optional)
  • Credits (Optional - defaults to 3)
💡 Tip: The system will automatically sort your data by Year and Semester after upload.
